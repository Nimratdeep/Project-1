Text Formatting

Text formatting helps in making the text look slightly different.

Following are the examples.

Strong makes the text look bold.
em makes the text italic.
Short makes the text shorter.
del makes the text look deleted. A line crossing the text basically.
ins underlines the text.
Mark highlihts the text.
sub makes the text look lower as compared to the remaining text.
sup helps the text look raised as compared to the remaining text.

div and span tags

Div can be used for styling. Div is used for larger chunks while span is used for smaller portions. Span is used with in-line elements while div is used with block-level content.

Hyperlinks Anchor

Anchor Attributes
_blank opens the URL in new browsing context.
_self opens the URL in the same browsing context.
_parent opens the URL in parent browsing context.
_top opens the URL into top-level browsing context.
